Justin Applefield

Hello! My name is Justin. I am an aspiring creator from West Bloomfield, Michigan.

I graduated in 2018 from Cranbrook Kingswood, and am currently a sophomore at the University of Michigan, where I am studying Computer Science in the College of Engineering, Business Adminstration in the Ross School of Business, and Mandarin Chinese. I plan to pursue a Bachelor of Science in Engineering and Bachelor of Business Administration dual degree. At Michigan, I am currently involved with Kol HaKavod Acapella, serving as the Musical Director, and with TAMID Group, an organization that aims to cultivate business leaders with experiential learning through business in Israel, serving as the Co-Director of Marketing for the Michigan Chapter.

I have had the opportunity to work on a few cool projects in the past couple of years. In June 2017, I developed a curriculum to foster interest in the English language among 3rd-5th graders, and traveled with peers to Guiyang No. 4 Experimental Primary School in Guiyang, Guizhou, China as part of an inaugural English teaching exchange program. In April 2018 I was an emcee at the VEX Robotics World Championship in Louisville, Kentucky, calling matches for the Spirit Division. In May 2018 I worked with the Cranbrook Kingswood Science Department and MIT's Internet of Things Lab to design a curriculum to teach 9th grade physics students about the Internet of Things with a hands-on project. In July-August 2018 I traveled to 7 countries in Asia over a 28 day period and produced a vlog every single day. In Fall 2018 I led the development team of "Metro Master," a game designed to help visually impaired students get comfortable with navigational skills, which was produced for "Gaming for the Greater Good," an introductory Computer Science class at the University of Michigan.

As you can probably tell, I have a wide array of interests including music, international business, and technology. If you want to collaborate on a project or just to chat, feel free to contact me at: justin@applefield.me
