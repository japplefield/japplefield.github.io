Justin Applefield

Hello! My name is Justin. I am a software engineer and creator from West Bloomfield, Michigan. I graduated in 2018 from Cranbrook Kingswood, and gratuated from the University of Michigan in 2022 with a Bachelor of Computer Science Engineering and a Bachelor of Business Administration. I also studied Mandarin Chinese. At Michigan, I was involved with Kol HaKavod Acapella and with TAMID Group.

In June 2017 I participated in a teaching exchange with Guiyang No. 4 Experimental Primary School in Guiyang, Guizhou, China. In 2018 I traveled to 7 countries in Asia over a 28 day period and produced a vlog every single day. In 2019 I studied at the Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. In 2020 I interned for Random Forest VC in Tel Aviv, Israel. In 2022 I interned for Duolingo in Pittsburgh, and I will be returning full time in 2023. I emceed the VEX Robotics World Championship in 2018 and 2022.
