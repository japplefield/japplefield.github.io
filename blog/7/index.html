Dubai Part 5: The Royal Scam
January 12, 2023
We woke up late and had some spaghetti for breakfast
We drove for about 90 minutes into the desert in Abu Dhabi
We got to the desert safari place, and were prepared to start dune bashing. It was a thrilling three minutes but then it ended abruptly. We were confused because the tour we booked was supposed to include 25-30 minutes of dune bashing. The driver confusingly told us to get out of the car back towards the camp and said to go ride the ATVs or the camels.
ATVs, Camels, sand boarding
Murt convinced them to let us go again on dune bashing. We named him Murt the Unscammable: dishonest people better watch out.
They served tea, the girls got henna, we sat down and there was a show: dancer and fire spinner; then food (not very good) and belly dancers. We left and got home much earlier than expected.
We made brownies for Murt's parents as a gift to thank them for their hospitality.
